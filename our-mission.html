Our Mission

At Medi-list, we are committed to providing high-quality healthcare services that are accessible and efficient for everyone. Our mission is to improve health outcomes by offering reliable and easy-to-navigate services tailored to your needs.

What is The Point of This?

Medi-list This engineering lifecycle project aims to solve the problem of streamlining all parts of the medical reconciliation process. The problem is that many patients (especially the elderly) have trouble remembering what medications they are on and how much of said medication they take. This often leads to confusion and extended time when speaking with their doctors/pharmacists. Medi-list is here to simplify your healthcare experience.
